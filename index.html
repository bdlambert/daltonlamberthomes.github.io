About Dalton Lambert Homes

Trusted craftsmanship in the Charlotte metro since day one.

Dalton Lambert Homes, LLC is a full-service residential construction company based in the Charlotte, North Carolina metro area. We specialize in custom home building, whole-home renovations, and construction project management.

Every project we take on reflects our commitment to quality materials, honest communication, and delivering on time. Whether you're building your dream home from the ground up or renovating an existing property, we bring the same level of care and attention to detail.

We believe that building a home should be a collaborative process. Our clients are involved every step of the way — from initial design discussions through final walkthrough.
